Scientific Symposium: Nanoplastics: Origin, Structure, and Fate

It is our great pleasure to announce that we will organize a scientific symposium: ‘Nanoplastics: Origin, Structure, and Fate’. The event will take place on November 20th and 21st 2023, in Utrecht, The Netherlands.

The aims of the symposium are to bring together (young) scientists from academia working in the field of nano- and microplastic research and to foster knowledge exchange. We therefore took care to construct a program for the symposium that fits this purpose. The first day of the symposium will start around noon and is dedicated to plenary lectures from experts in the field, such as yourself. The day will be closed with a poster session, dinner, and drinks. On the second day of the symposium, three interactive workshops are organised, related to the theme of the symposium; Origin, Structure, and Fate. We will end the symposium with a special networking event!

More information will be announced later. Registration will be opened soon.
